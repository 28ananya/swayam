Welocome to We Care Hospital


We Care Hospital since its inception in 1984 one of the pioneer private Hospital at Dhaka and as a matter fact all over Bangladesh. The Hospital is situated at the heart of capital Dhaka in Bashundhara. From day one till today it has not compromised on quality services and emerged as one of the best hospitals in public sector. At We Care, patient can have access to over 100 consultant doctors from 25 sub-specialities. Our dedicated team of health care professionals is committed to give each patient the best care. Keeping in mind the motto ‘In Life, We care’. We Care Hospital is serving for the last 32 years, providing both outdoor and indoor diagnostic services. We try to give the highest priority to the best possible medical care for our patient.
